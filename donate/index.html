NYC Mesh has big plans!

We've installed one rooftop sector antenna at a large data center. This has line of sight to downtown Manhattan and parts of Brooklyn. We plan to install more antennas to increase our range.

The "supernode" allows us to provide a gigabit of bandwidth to our existing network. This is a critical step in building a high capacity backbone to support further expansion.

But hardware, roof-rights and rack space cost money.
All told, we will need around $12,000 per year to maintain the supernode.
